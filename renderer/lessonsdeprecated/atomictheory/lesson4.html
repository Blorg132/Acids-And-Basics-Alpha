"The Atomic Theory "


"Side to side are periods wide, up and down are groups with pride."


In the periodic table of elements, elements are arranged in horizontal rows known as periods and vertical columns known as family/groups.

"Periods keep the shell count still, families add shells at will."

  • Period - When moving across the same period, no. of electron shells remains the same
  • Family - However, when moving down the same family, the no. of electron shells increase
