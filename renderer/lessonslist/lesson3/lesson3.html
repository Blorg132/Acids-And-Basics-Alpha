Physical vs Chemical Changes


In science, it is especially important to understand the difference between physical change & chemical changes as they can explain the many ways through which matter can transform. Furthermore, by learning to recognize such changes, natural processes can be better explained and connected to real-life application.

How to distinguish between physical & chemical changes


Properly distinguishing between physical and chemical changes can be challenging at times since some changes don't show simiar signs, whilst others have a complec nature. Such examples include

  • The dissolving of salt in water
  • The formation of alloys

Despite such challenges, there are many techniques that can be used to differentiate between the two. The following are some of the ways through which a physical chnage can be identified
  • Reversible in many cases
  • No new substance is formed
  • Only physical properties (shape, size, volume , and changes in phase)
While on the other hand, some of the ways through which chemical changes are identified
  • Change in color
  • The production of gas bubbles
  • The release or absorption of heat
